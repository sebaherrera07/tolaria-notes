# Weekly Review
A step-by-step process for reviewing your week and planning ahead. Run this every Friday or Sunday.
## Steps
1. **Clear the Inbox** — Process all notes in your Tolaria inbox. Mark as organized or archive.   2. **Review Active Projects** — Check progress on all active projects. Update status if needed.
3. **Check Responsibilities** — What needs attention this week? Any pending follow-ups?
4. **Review Calendar** — Look at next week's events. Prepare for any important meetings.
5. **Collect Loose Notes** — Review any new notes created this week. Link to related topics.
6. **Plan Next Week** — Create or update notes for what you want to accomplish.## Time Estimate
30-60 minutes depending on how much accumulated.
