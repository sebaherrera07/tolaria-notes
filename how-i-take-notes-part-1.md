# How I Take Notes — Part 1

Luca's essay on his personal note- taking system, covering the PRT (Progress, Reflection, Things) framework and how he organizes his knowledge base.
## Summary
The essay explains Luca's approach to note-taking, emphasizing:
## Key Points
- Notes should capture ideas at the moment of inspiration
- Separate capture from organization (the inbox concept)
- Using types as navigation lenses, not rigid schemas
- The value of linking notes to create a knowledge graph
- Why plain text/markdown matters for long-term storage
This essay forms the basis for many of Tolaria's design decisions. If you want to understand why Tolaria works the way it does, start here.
